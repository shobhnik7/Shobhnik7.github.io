Shobhnik Kriplani

Hi! I'm an Undergrad student in Math & Computing at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. My current research interests are AI for Science, Reasoning in LLMs, and AI Safety.

I recently completed a Research Internship at Virginia Tech, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Chandan K. Reddy and collaborated with Sanchit Kabra on SurfaceBench, a large-scale benchmark for evaluating symbolic regression models.

Before that, I worked on AI Alignment at SAFERR AI Lab. Collaborating with Souradip Chakraborty and Prof. Amrit Singh Bedi.

I also worked at Stochastic Machine Learning Lab. I primarily collaborated with Adith Muralidharan and Prof. Shishir N.Y.K. I focused on visual perception module for robotic navigation and Developed an offline-to-online RL pipeline to enhance motion planning.

I started my Research journey at DACAS Lab, where I worked under the guidance of Prof. Jishnu Keshavan. I Developed a framework to solve Robot Kinematics using Invertible Neural Networks(INNs), achieving significant Improvement in accurancy and Inference Time.

I'm always excited to explore new ideas. Feel free to send me an email at Shobhnikk [at] iisc.ac.in
